Cyber Knight

Author: Reila Arvier

Genre: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Published: xxxx

Synopsis: Rheala is one of the last surviving units of the outlawed program known as Peony. As the final test subject created by the Peony researchers, she was discovered in a cryogenic tube by Jake—a former Cyber Knight turned mercenary. Now known by the codename λ-03, Jake fights as part of the revolutionary faction known as The Ashen Vanguard, a group determined to overthrow the oppressive regime of Neo Light Nexus.

Together, Rheala, Jake, and the Ashen Vanguard strive to dismantle the corrupt governors who erected the towering walls of Neo Light Nexus, dividing the privileged core from the desolate outskirts. Their mission is also a reckoning with the legacy of the Hollow Flux, a catastrophic event from ten years ago that continues to haunt the city and its people.
